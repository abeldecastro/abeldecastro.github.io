Unity Path Consulting LLC

Empowering individuals, professional athletes, and organizations in the United States through financial education, innovative planning, and personalized investment strategies.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower individuals and organizations in the United States through financial education, innovative planning, and personalized investment strategies, with a specialized focus on professional athletes and underserved communities, fostering long-term financial stability and growth.

Vision

To become a leading financial consulting and education platform in the United States, recognized for transforming how individuals and professional athletes understand, manage, and grow their wealth—building a culture of financial literacy that strengthens both personal prosperity and the national economy.

Core Services

Financial Education Center

Our flagship initiative dedicated to advancing financial literacy nationwide through workshops, seminars, online courses, and community partnerships. Programs cover budgeting, investments, retirement planning, and tax-efficient strategies, with bilingual delivery in English, Portuguese, and Spanish.

Financial Planning for Professional Athletes

Specialized financial planning for athletes across professional leagues, collegiate programs, and international transfers. Services focus on wealth preservation, risk management, tax compliance, and career transition planning.

Investment Education & Market Training

Educational programs designed to teach responsible investing, risk assessment, and long-term wealth-building strategies using both digital platforms and in-person instruction.

Customized Investment Solutions

Tailored investment strategies aligned with each client’s objectives and risk tolerance, emphasizing diversified and responsible asset allocation with continuous monitoring.

Corporate Advisory & Internal Controls

Advisory services for corporations focused on internal controls, governance, compliance frameworks, and strategic financial decision-making to enhance resilience and efficiency.

Unique Business Model

Financial Education as a Public Good

Education is a core mission of Unity Path Consulting. By addressing financial illiteracy, the firm contributes to reducing household debt, improving retirement readiness, and empowering underserved communities—issues of national importance in the United States.

Athlete-Centric Financial Planning

A dedicated practice focused on professional athletes, addressing the systemic challenges of short career spans, income volatility, and post-retirement financial distress through structured, compliant, and long-term planning strategies.
